## AI Automation & Voice Agent Engineer

I build AI systems that automate lead follow-ups, qualify prospects, and handle communication at scale — so businesses stop losing deals due to slow or missed responses.

## What I Build

**AI Voice Agents**
Inbound/outbound agents that qualify leads, book appointments, and follow up automatically.
*Stack:* Vapi · ElevenLabs · Twilio · OpenAI

**LLM-Powered Workflows**
RAG systems, AI assistants, and chatbots trained on business data.
*Stack:* LangChain · OpenAI · Pinecone · FastAPI

**Automation Systems**
End-to-end workflows that replace manual operations.
*Stack:* n8n · Make.com · Webhooks

**CRM & Communication**
Fully connected systems across messaging, CRM, and APIs.
*Stack:* Twilio · GoHighLevel · HubSpot · REST APIs
